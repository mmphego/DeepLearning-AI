In general, the number of neurons in the previous layer gives us the number of columns of the weight matrix, and the number of neurons in the current layer gives us the number of rows in the weight matrix.

Building blocks of deep neural networks

A key takeaway is that, when building a basic building blocks for implementing a deep neural network, in each layer there's a forward propagation step and there's a corresponding backpropagation step as well as a cache (z[l−1])(z^{[l-1]})(z[l−1])﻿ to pass the information from one layer to another.
